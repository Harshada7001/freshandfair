Blessed with moderate climate & agriculture-friendly topography, Nashik, Maharahtra is a key player in the domain of Indian agro-exports.

The farmers in Nasik are very progressive and follow advanced farming pratices to achieve and maintain world quality standards.

Nashik has more than 1.75 lakh hectares of grape cultivation & accounts for 55% share of overall Indian export of table grapes. Likewise Nashik accounts for majority of the overall onion export of India & has a significant share in other fresh fruits & vegetables also.

Despite these encouraging statistics, farmers face some inherent challenges due to practical constraints like moderate land holding, manpower availability due to seasonal nature of business, access to global markets & a few other challenges.

Similarly importers also require reliable sources who have a logistics infrastructure & an excellent reach with the farmer fraternity & are aware of the global standards & trade practices.

Fresh & Fair with its unique approach is the conduit to promote high quality, reliable & ethical trade globally.
