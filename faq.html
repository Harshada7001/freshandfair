Frequently Asked Questions

We aim high at being focused on building relationships with our clients and community.

How will you assure traceability and transparency in payments?
We use cloud base ERP software to give complete traceability and transparency.
What if farmer produce does not meet supermarkets quality specifications?
The field supervisor of Fresh & Fair is continuosly guiding the farmers on crop quality. Shortfalls in quality requirements are brought to the notice of farmers immdiately. Crop which does not qualify to the specifications will not harvested by Fresh & Fair. This ensures that only the fruit conforming to the quality specifications reaches the supermarkets.
Does company show final account sales to farmers?
Yes, Fresh & Fair shows final account sales of a particular container only to respective farmers whose produce is packed in that particular container.
Does the company provide advance amount to registered farmers?
No, company does not provide any advances to farmers. Company will transfer the payments to farmers account only after receipt from importers.
What if registered farmer wants to give his non-registered plots to company?
As far as possible company will not harvest non-registered plots but if there is additional requirement from importers then these plots will be harvested. In such cases, Fresh & Fair will ensure that the quality norms prescribed by the importer are not compromised.
Does company charge additional certification fees and lab analysis cost to JV farmers apart from declared cost per box.
No, company does not charge any additional cost of certification and lab analysis from JV farmers. This cost is already included in fixed cost declared per box before the season starts.
Though, this cost will have to be paid for fixed-rate famers.
Does farmer need to register with the company every year ?
Yes, farmer has to renew his registration with the company every year.
